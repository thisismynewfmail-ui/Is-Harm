CASE FILE: THE RAPE GANG INQUIRY PERIOD: 1955–2026 SCOPE: 149 DISTRICTS · UNITED KINGDOM STATUS: ONGOING

They knew.They let it happen.

For seventy years, in at least 149 towns and cities, organised gangs groomed, raped and trafficked children. The report this site is built on concludes that every institution that should have stopped it — police, social services, the NHS, schools, councils, courts — chose not to. This is its evidence. All of it. Rebuilt so it cannot be skimmed, shelved, or forgotten.

An unofficial, art-format exposition of the full published report of the Rape Gang Inquiry — the non-statutory, crowdfunded inquiry chaired by Rupert Lowe MP, its team led by survivor Sammy Woodhouse. Every claim on this page is the report's, reproduced and attributed. Nothing has been softened, and nothing has been added.
